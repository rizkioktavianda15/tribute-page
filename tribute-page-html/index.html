Abdrurrahman Wahid

Pahlawan Reformasi Indonesia

Here's a time line of Dr. Abdurrahman Wahid

  • 1940- Lahir di Denanyar, Jombang, Jawa Timur
  • 1963- Receive a Scholarship at Al Azhar University in Kairo, Egypt.
  • 1971- Back to Indonesia after finishing they Scholarship
  • 1974- Working as Teacher at Pesantren Tambakberas
  • 1977- Joined Hasyim Asy'ari as a Lecturer
  • 1984- Elected as Leader of Nahdathul Ulama
  • 1989- Second time as Leader of NU and Opposing the New Orde Politic
  • 1994- Third time as Leader of NU and Lead-up to Reformasi
  • 1998- Winning Reformasi
  • 1999- Elected as the President after Suharto resignment
  • 2009- dies at age 69
